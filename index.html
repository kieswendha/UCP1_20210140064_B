Days Off

In my daily routine, I experience two distinct categories: working days and days off. These categories bring contrasting experiences and activities, each with its own significance. During a working day, my schedule is structured around my job or occupation. On the other hand, a day off is a welcome break from work. It is a day to relax, recharge, and pursue personal interests and hobbies.

Working Days

Previous Next

On a typical workday, my life is filled with trendy activities. I start with a delicious bowl of chicken porridge for breakfast. Then, I attend classes on campus, followed by a satisfying lunch break. I stay active in student organizations, complete assignments, and indulge in some retail therapy at Mirota. Finally, I enjoy a dinner at an angkringan, embracing the local culture. Each day is vibrant and dynamic, allowing me to create lasting memories.

Days Off

Previous Next

On a day off, my activities are filled with trendy and exciting experiences. I start by waking up early and opening the curtains to let in the morning light. Playing with my cat brings me joy and laughter. I embark on a car journey, enjoying breakfast on the go. Off-roading with my cousin adds a thrilling touch to the day. We take a ride on a Ferris wheel and go rafting, feeling the rush of adrenaline. A delicious spaghetti lunch refuels me for more adventures. Capturing memories at the hotel and swimming with my cousin create special moments. Quality time with my family is precious, followed by a healthy salad dinner. Finally, I prepare for a restful sleep, grateful for the memorable day.
